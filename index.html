DataMines is your help to find valuable data and mine it, using the latest algorithms from AI and Machine Learning.



Vision

Data mining is an ongoing innovative transition into a new way of collecting, handling and extracting information from data for many companies. It requires knowledge about AI, Machine Learning and traditional statistical analysis tools to be successful, but often this alone is not enough. Data mining might also require exploring High Performance Computing and Cloud Computing, as well as hosting solutions. DataMines helps customers achieve results by increasing their knowledge within data-driven methods and providing users with state-of-the-art analysis algorithms at their hands.

My name is David Malmgren-Hansen and DataMines is my consultancy. I have delivered solutions in AI and Machine Learning algorithm development to diverse customers base since November 2017. I hold a PhD from DTU in an industrial project with Terma A/S about Deep Learning/AI algorithms for satellite- and airborne sensor data. Though I have experience in programming in several common languages, my key expertise lies in developing data-driven algorithms, whether it is AI-based, Machine Learning models or classical statistical methods. I also have experience with classical Computer Vision techniques from mathematical describing camera setups to extracting geometric information.
